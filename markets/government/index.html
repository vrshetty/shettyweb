Government

Defend critical infrastructure, uncover blind spots and bridge interagency gaps.

Defense, intelligence and law-enforcement agencies in the USA and around the world trust Norse to deliver unique visibility into cyberattacks emanating from darknets and the most difficult-to-penetrate geographies.

In federal contexts, the responsibility for cyberattack intelligence collection in the United States is shared across DoD, DHS and the DOJ. This often creates unintended gaps and “blind spots”. Furthermore, federal entities in the US (and allied governments alike) are often constrained by regulations that make fast, effective intelligence sharing difficult, while adversaries ranging from organized crime to terrorist cells to nation states are increasingly targeting critical infrastructure systems. Federal customers in the USA and allied nations around the world rely on Norse intelligence offerings to bridge those gaps and deliver increased visibility into difficult-to-penetrate geographies and darknets.
